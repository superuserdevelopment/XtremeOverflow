Who are we?

IEEEXtreme is a flagship event for our community, and is recognized world-wide. But, people who aren’t well-versed with coding feel intimidated by the scale of the competition. Our competition is conceptualized to be a stepping stone to IEEEXtreme. We aim to assist participants to learn and ease into competitive coding effectively.

Flow of the Event

  1. Start with interactive webinars and tutorials from seasoned competitive programmers and experts in the field.
  2. Learn using the weekly study material and practice the programs provided to your regularly through our Telegram Channel.
  3. Apply the skills you gain over the 5 weeks in the coding competitions hosted every week and test your metal in the Grand Finale to win cash prizes upto Rs.10,000.
