FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is XTREME OVERFLOW?

Xtreme Overflow is a 5 week long event organized by 5 IEEE Student Branches in association with IEEE Bombay Section as a way to prepare students for IEEE Xtreme an annual programming competition.

I am new to Competitive Programming, how will this benefit me?

The goal of this event is to promote Competitive Programming as an sport. Even for beginners we will be providing complete support from start to the end of the event. This is not just about the contests, but about 5 weeks of learning that the candidate will benefit from.

What are the prizes?

The prizes for the winners are as follows:

1st Prize Rs. 5000*/-
2nd Prize Rs. 2000*/-
3rd Prize Rs. 1000*/-

Please note that the ranking will be obtained on cumulative scores of all 5 weeks and the respective contests held. To win the participant must be consitent throughout the 5 weeks.

Which platform is being used?

This contest is going to be hosted on HackerRank. The updates for the same will be provided through our Telegram Channel and the Website.

I'am not an IEEE member, can I participate?                                     

This event is free for everyone to participate in. An IEEE Membership of any kind is not required to participate in this event.
